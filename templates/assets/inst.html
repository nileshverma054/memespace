Welcome to Memespace Event

Rules:

1. Number of members in a team should not exceed 2.
2. The event will be of two rounds.
3. Usage of any browsing site or refreshing the page is strictly prohibited and if done so test will be submitted.

Round 1:

It will consists of two types:

1. The time alloted for this round is 10 minutes.
2. Your responses will not be considered as final unless you click on SUBMIT button.
3. For answering NEXT question ,Click on NEXT button.
4. Identify the memes and enter the missing text.
5. Tags should be selected from given options.
6. The score will be decided by checking the number of words guessed correctly.
7. The teams with highest scores will qualify for round 2.

Round 2:

1. Create memes based on a given topic.
2. The time alloted for this round is 30 minutes.
3. The memes which are created will be judged based on relevance to the given
topic, creativity, and the meme format used.
